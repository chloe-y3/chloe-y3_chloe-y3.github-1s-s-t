I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Algorithms, Combinatorics, and Optimization (ACO) at Georgia Institute of Technology, based in the School of Industrial and Systems Engineering. I am fortunate to be advised by Jamie Morgenstern. My main research interests are in Fairness, Mechanism Design and Algorithmic Game Theory.

Before attending Georgia Institute of Technology, I spent six months at EconCS group at Harvard University, under the supervision of Professor Yiling Chen.

Prior to that, I completed my undergraduate studies in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the guidance of Professor Xiaotie Deng.
